How to use FLASH Web Wallet

Flash Web Wallet

Why use a web wallet? Because you want all the great Flash features without having to use the desktop core wallet and download the entire blockchain (which can take a long time and use up your computer memory).

To begin, visit the web wallet page: https://www.flashcoin.io/#web-wallet

1. Click on the ‘Flash Web Wallet‘ or ‘Get a web wallet here’ buttons.

2. On the registration page, enter your name and email address, and select Create Wallet.

3. Verify your email by going to your email inbox and continue to login to the Flash Web Wallet. Your main login page will look like this once successful.

4. You should consider enabling 2FA (two-factor authentication); this helps to further secure your wallet and is done through the Google Authenticator app. It is not mandatory to activate 2FA to use the wallet but it is highly advised. To activate, go to your My Account tab and then click on Account Settings. Scan your QR code with your phone using the app (explained in the step below).

Write down or store your recovery key somewhere safe in case you ever lose your phone.

Pay attention that there is no way to recover the wallet without the recovery key or the 2FA

5. Follow the illustrated steps for the Google Authenticator app to add the Flash Web Wallet 2FA.

6. Now you can enter the code you received in the app to sign in to your account.




Flash Web Wallet

1. To receive Flash coins, you will need to provide the sender with your Flash coin Address or your QR code. To identify your Public Address and QR code, click on the My Account tab and then select Account Settings. Your Public Address can be used to receive Flash coins or alternatively, your QR code can be scanned by the sender to send you Flash coins.

2. To send Flash coins, select the Send tab and input the Flash address or scan the QR code. Input the amount you wish to send and click Continue. Your transaction will require one more confirmation and then your coins will be sent. (Please note, once coins have been sent you cannot reverse the transaction. Hence it is necessary you are sending to the correct address and to someone whom you trust.)

3. Your Sent/Received transactions can be viewed by clicking on the Activity tab.

Additional Features of the Flash Web Wallet

Contacts
When you send a payment, the user will be added automatically to your contact list so you will not need to re-enter their Address for future transactions.

Flashcoin Fountain

The Flashcoin fountain is a reward system, which can be implemented on a website or app, to dispense rewards in the form of Flash coins, for visitors to claim in exchange for completing a captcha or task as described by the website. It can be accessed by going to your Account Settings tab and clicking on Fountain. There are many benefits of the fountain such as:

  1. Introducing new users to Flash coin
  2. Generating traffic to your site or
  3. Making money by adding additional content to the site
