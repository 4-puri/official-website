About FLASH

What is FLASH?

FLASH is a reinvention of Litecoin, built to scale for worldwide commerce and fast enough to handle everyday transactions. The flexible and easy to integrate core code allows exchanges and wallets to add FLASH to their platform within hours. With a settlement time of around 5 seconds and consensus within 2 minutes, anyone, anywhere can use the FLASH mobile wallet as easily as cash or credit card.

What is it for?

FLASH is designed to make it easy, inexpensive, and fast to transfer value worldwide. Imagine buying grocercies, purchasing products online, and sending FLASH to friends and family in the blink of an eye, anywhere in the world, all for way is less than a penny.

Who can use it?

Anyone can use FLASH, as it was designed for the common person and doesn't require a middleman, like a bank. FLASH remittance payments are nearly instant and with fees that are near zero, anyone can send FLASH worldwide or buy products or services locally with unparalledled convenience.

The FLASH Advantage

No Unfair Advantage

Everyone donated something for their FLASH, except for the tiny amount of FLASH that was given to people who originally tested the platform. There is no developer slush fund, just a strong community behind FLASH.

Zero Inflation

All coins were issued to the community. No new coins will ever be issued. If you want some FLASH you will need to trade with community members or purchase it on an exchange.

Public blockchain

Public blockchain operated for the public good. FLASH is a not for profit organization that has cut out the middleman to enable fast and inexpensive transactions.

Community supported

Anyone can develop on the FLASH blockchain. Build a project and decide how much you want to charge, while enabling direct payments to your FLASH wallet.

Eco-Friendly

FLASH doesn’t require energy intensive mining to create new coins because the entire supply is already in circulation.

High volume

FLASH focuses on high transaction volume because the ultimate goal is to support worldwide adoption.

Roadmap

FLASH is being optimized to support applications in the $1 Trillion remittance market. We have exchanges in UAE, India, Mexico and Liechtenstein that have agreed to participate.

Features

The features are broken into several categories:
  • FLASH web wallet (FWW), which will have support for credit card purchase and in-wallet exchange for BTC, BCH, and local currency. Many additional local languages will be added
  • Blockchain upgrades to make FLASH more stable
  • Remittance applications including Human ATM and automatic conversion to local fiat
  • FLASH Merchant services including consumer payment via FLASH with cash back feature
Please Note: Roadmap will be updated on regular basis as we make progress.

Exchanges

FLASH supports any legal cryptocurrency exchange, including decentralized exchanges. Our community occasionally contributes funds to get FLASH listed. FLASH is ideal for exchanges, as the transfer speeds and transaction costs are very low and because it is based on LTC, integration. If you operate an exchange and are interested in listing FLASH, please contact support@flashcoin.io to receive a file with details on our source code repo for exchanges and a legal opinion regarding FLASH’s status as a security token (it’s a community coin, not a security)
